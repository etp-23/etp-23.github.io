Introduction

Sexist language is pervasive in recent social media platforms. The core objective of our research centers on the identification and transformation of sexist language within user-generated comments across widely used social media platforms, thereby contributing to the promotion of tolerant and respectful discourse. This research is distinctly based on data in English language. In this report, we expect to furnish an exposition of the corpus to be employed for training our deep learning models. Furthermore, we aim to outline the multifaceted approaches we intend to employ to address the issue of sexist language. Moreover, we will undertake a conscientious exploration of the ethical dimensions of our project.
